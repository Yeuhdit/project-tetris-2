Tetris Game Instructions

What's the goal?

Arrange the falling blocks to complete full rows without gaps.

Each full row disappears and scores points – the more rows you clear, the higher you go!

How to play?

  • Move the block with the left and right arrow keys.
  • Press the up arrow to rotate the block.
  • The blocks fall faster over time – be quick and sharp!

What happens when you reach the top of the screen?

Game over. Try again and beat your score!

Who wins?

The winner is the one who scores the most points before the game ends.

Tips to play better

  • Plan where to place the blocks to avoid gaps.
  • Keep space open for easy block rotation.
  • Stay focused as the speed increases to score high!
